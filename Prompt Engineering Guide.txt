Prompting is the process of providing specific instructions to a
generative AI tool to receive new information or to achieve a
desired outcome on a task.

Some examples:
• Text
• Images
• Videos
• Sound
• Code

5 Step Framework
Task: what you want the AI to do.

Prompt examples:
Suggest a birthday gift for my friend who enjoys reading.

These prompts can be elevated so that we get a result back that
is more unique and specific by:
1. Including a persona which is a role that you want the AI to
   embody.

   Act as a librarian and book recommendation expert.
   Suggest a birthday gift for my friend who enjoys reading.

   This should make the results be more specific.
2. Including the format of the output will make the results more
   structured.

   Act as a librarian and book recommendation expert.
   Suggest a birthday gift for my friend who enjoys reading.

   Format your response using the following sections:

   1. Gift Idea
   2. Why It Is a Good Choice
   3. Estimated Cost
   4. Personalisation Ideas
